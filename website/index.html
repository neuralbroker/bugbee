01

Local by default

Index and deterministic detection stay in the repository. Sensitive paths are blocked and model-bound content is redacted.

02

Evidence before action

Every finding carries location, source/sink context, confidence, risk scoring, and a reviewable SARIF export.

03

Humans hold the keys

Bugbee proposes and records. Your team confirms findings and controls every production change.
